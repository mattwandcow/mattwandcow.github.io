Article Title

Discovered by a local drilling team in 1937, the Soda Springs Geyser has far surpassed the potential swimming pool as an attraction to Soda Springs. Unlike Old Faithful, the geyser is not self regulating, so an artifical cap was installed. The geyser fires on the hour, every hour.

It has become a symbol for the town, the center of it's tourism, and it has a nice park celebrating this mixture of nature's power and mankind's ingenuity.
